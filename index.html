Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Important: This privacy policy complies with Apple App Store requirements and describes how QuestrAI handles camera, photo library, and image data.

This Privacy Policy describes how QuestrAI collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our iOS application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication provider data (Google, Apple, or email/password)
  • User ID (anonymous identifier)

1.2 Camera & Photos Access

With your permission, we access:

  • Camera: To scan documents and capture images for translation
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos for scanning/translation

Note: We only access photos you explicitly select. We do not scan your entire photo library.

1.3 Image & Text Data

When you use scanning or translation features:

  • Images you capture/select (processed temporarily)
  • Text extracted from images via Optical Character Recognition (OCR)
  • Translated text in your chosen language
  • Translated images you choose to download

1.4 Chat & AI Data

  • Your chat questions and AI responses
  • Important messages you save
  • Chat context for conversation continuity

1.5 Subscription Information

  • Purchase history via Apple App Store
  • Subscription status and expiration dates
  • Trial usage information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide scanning, translation, and AI chat functionality
  • To process images and extract text for translation
  • To generate translated versions of images
  • To process subscriptions and prevent fraud
  • To improve AI responses using chat context
  • To send password reset emails when requested
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Processing & Third-Party Services

3.1 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication (sign-in services)
  • Cloud Firestore (data storage)
  • Cloud Functions (server-side processing)

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 DeepSeek AI

Your chat questions are sent to DeepSeek API to generate AI responses.

3.3 Apple Vision & Translation

For scanning and translation features, we use Apple's:

  • VisionKit: For text recognition in images (OCR)
  • Apple Translation API: For multilingual text translation

All image processing occurs locally on your device. No images or text are sent to Apple's servers.

3.4 Apple App Store

For subscription purchases, we use Apple's in-app purchase system.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

4.1 Image Data

  • Original images are processed temporarily and not stored permanently
  • Translated images are stored only if you choose to download them to your device
  • We do not maintain a permanent copy of your images on our servers

4.2 Account Data

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data by:

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Camera/Photo Access: You can enable/disable in iOS Settings
  • Data Access: View your data in the app
  • Data Deletion: Delete your account anytime
  • Data Export: Contact us to export your data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure Firebase authentication
  • Temporary image processing (images not stored long-term)
  • Regular security reviews

8. Photo Library & Camera Usage

Our app requests access to:

  • Camera: To capture new images for scanning/translation
  • Photo Library: To select existing images for processing

We only access specific photos you choose. We do not access your entire photo library.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
